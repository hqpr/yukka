About Yukka

What is Yukka? Yukka sells the freshest fashion, whether you're into U.S. labels, exclusive European streetwear, the best of British independents or following the latest style clippings from the likes of Kanye West, Jay-Z or Rihanna then Yukka should be your first point of call.

Established in 2003, Yukka was born out of the streets desire for urban biased fashion. When Dre, Eminem and 50 Cent were causing waves in the early 2000's the fashion of hip hop and urban clothing came to the fore in a big way...with no retailers around to provide for this genre Yukka was born, kicking off by selling the likes of G-Unit, RocaWear, Ecko, Akademiks and Sean John and a whole host of other fresh brands.

From there a streetwear movement grew to what it is now, Yukka with over 100 brands and 2500 products caters for the streetwear lifestyle market. Read More...
