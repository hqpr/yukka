THE YUKKA SIZE GUIDE

Yukka stocks over 2500 items at any given time from over 100 or so brands that arrive from all over the world, places like New York, Los Angeles, Paris, Barcelona, London. Because of the variety of goods we sell we've always found that some brands 'fit' differently to others - this is entirely down to the look the brand is created for. So if you're buying into an Urban brand like Ecko or RocaWear that the Hip Hop community tend to follow sizing tends to be baggier, Street brands like Adidas Originals, Ringspun, Money and so on tend to be a regular UK fit and Skate brands like DC Shoes, Zoo York tend to come somewhere in between!

To make your life a little easier on each product page under 'ADDITIONAL INFORMATION' we've defined a 'LOOK' to help you figure out what the fit is like on that item, so:

URBAN - A Baggier, More Relaxed Look
STREET - A normal UK fit, slightly more fitted but not too tight!
SKATE - Somewhere between 'Street' and 'Urban', designed to be a relaxed fit

About Yukka

What is Yukka? Yukka sells the freshest fashion, whether you're into U.S. labels, exclusive European streetwear, the best of British independents or following the latest style clippings from the likes of Kanye West, Jay-Z or Rihanna then Yukka should be your first point of call.

Established in 2003, Yukka was born out of the streets desire for urban biased fashion. When Dre, Eminem and 50 Cent were causing waves in the early 2000's the fashion of hip hop and urban clothing came to the fore in a big way...with no retailers around to provide for this genre Yukka was born, kicking off by selling the likes of G-Unit, RocaWear, Ecko, Akademiks and Sean John and a whole host of other fresh brands.

From there a streetwear movement grew to what it is now, Yukka with over 100 brands and 2500 products caters for the streetwear lifestyle market. Read More...
